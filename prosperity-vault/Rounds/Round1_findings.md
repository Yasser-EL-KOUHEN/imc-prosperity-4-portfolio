## ASH_COATED_OSMIUM (ACO)

### EDA Findings

- **AR(1) coefficient:** ρ₁ = −0.495 (strong mean-reversion on tick-to-tick changes)
- **FV mean:** 10,000 (essentially constant; std ≈ 5/day)
- **Bot spread:** 16 ticks (bot quotes near 9992/10008)
- **Regime:** Mean-reverting; Hurst < 0.5

### Strategy (v3 — DEVELOPED, NOT SUBMITTED to R1)

> ⚠️ **The strategy described in this section is v3, which was NOT submitted to R1.** v3 was developed in the R1→R2 gap and first submitted to R2. **R1's realized $98,172 came from v2** — book-anchored quotes only, *without* the FV anchor blend below. v2 used `best_bid+1 / best_ask-1` book-anchored placement with the simpler EMA-only FV (no anchor toward 10,000). The v3 logic below is preserved here as the documented R2 strategy. See [[Performance/Submission_Verification]].

**Fair value (v3, R2-onward):**
```
FV_base = 0.85 × EMA(mid, α=0.50) + 0.15 × 10,000
FV      = FV_base − ρ(|Δmid|) × Δmid
```

**Magnitude-bucketed reversion coefficient ρ (v3):**

| |Δmid| | ρ    |
|---------|------|
| < 2     | 0.25 |
| 2–5     | 0.60 |
| ≥ 5     | 0.74 |

**Quote placement (shared between v2 and v3 — book-anchored is what made R1 work):**
- Passive: `best_bid+1` / `best_ask-1` (1 tick inside bot's spread — price priority over bot)
- Aggressive: take any ask < FV or bid > FV

**Real exchange Day 0:** 2,668 XIREC (this is the v2 R1 number; v3 was never run in R1)

### v1 Bug / v2 Fix

The v1 passive quotes were computed as `FV ± QUOTE_OFFSET`. Since FV ≈ 10,000 and the bot spread is 16 ticks, our bid would be at 9994 (inside the bot's 9992) and ask at 10006 (inside 10008). But time priority means the bot's quotes fill first — our quotes never reach the front of the queue. Jasper's `--match-trades=all` mode ignored this, giving a falsely high score.

v2 fix: anchor to `best_bid+1 / best_ask-1` regardless of FV — always guarantee price priority.

## INTARIAN_PEPPER_ROOT (IPR)

### EDA Findings

- **Trend:** +0.1 XIREC per tick = +1,000/day (perfectly linear)
- **Regime:** Trending (momentum; opposite of ACO)

### Strategy

1. Aggressively take all available asks each tick (up to buy_cap)
2. Post passive buy at `best_bid+1` for remaining capacity
3. **Never post sells** — hold long position for all 3 days

## What Worked

1. **Book-anchored passive quotes (v2)** — price priority guarantees fills ahead of the bot
2. **FV anchor blend (v3)** — Bayesian shrinkage toward known prior eliminates EMA noise; +4.1% on ACO
3. **Greedy IPR accumulation** — reached max long quickly, captured 99.2% of the trend ceiling
4. **EDA-informed regime identification** — confirmed mean-reversion vs trending before choosing strategy

## What Didn't Work

1. **v1 FV-offset quotes** — never fill in practice (Jasper bug masked this)
2. **Inventory skew on ACO** — loses passive-fill edge, rejected by per-day anti-overfit gate
3. **Inventory gate** (rho downscale when loaded) — AR(1) is still predictive at any position
