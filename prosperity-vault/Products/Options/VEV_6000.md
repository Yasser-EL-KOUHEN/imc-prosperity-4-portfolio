## Phase 12 Investigation: The "Sell at 0" Trap

Phase 12 RESEARCH proposed **Change A**: sell at `best_bid = 0` to match Mark 22's behaviour and capture the 317 fills/day they were getting. Implementation tested and **REVERTED**. Reasons:

1. Local backtester settles positions daily at `mid = (bid + ask)/2 = 0.5`. Selling 300 units (position cap) × 0.5 = **−150/day per product MTM loss** = −900 over 3 days.
2. True BS value (S=5250, K=6000, T=7d, σ=0.24) ≈ 0.02 per unit. Local backtest overestimates settlement cost by 25×.
3. Even on the live server with correct settlement, EV = 0 (sell at 0, collect 0 premium, expire worthless). No upside.
4. Mark 22 sells at 0 as part of an unknown hedged portfolio strategy we cannot replicate.

**Decision:** keep posting at ask=1 (no fill, 0 PnL exposure) rather than at bid=0 (uncertain MTM, EV=0).
