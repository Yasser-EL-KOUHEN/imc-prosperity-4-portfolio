## ATM — The Vol Smile Trough

VEV_5400 sits at the **trough of the implied volatility smile**:
- σ=0.230 vs σ=0.245 for VEV_5300 and σ=0.249 for VEV_5500
- This is typical of equity options vol smiles — ATM has lower implied vol than OTM
- The pattern: IV surface shows a skew with higher vol for deep OTM and deep ITM

**ML analogy:** The vol smile is like a non-parametric uncertainty envelope around the BS model. ATM options have the most liquid price discovery, so the model is "most confident" there; OTM options price in tail risk with extra vol.
